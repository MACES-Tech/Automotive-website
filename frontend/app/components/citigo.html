Skoda Citigo

The ŠKODA CITIGO redefines what’s possible in a city car. The compact dimensions conceal a surprisingly generous interior with top workmanship and numerous Simply Clever solutions, making the stylish-yet-thrifty CITIGO a very safe, mature all-rounder that is well suited to the city and beyond. It’s perfect for young drivers and families, couples, or as an additional runaround.
